# AtlasBoard Product Overview

AtlasBoard is a fictional B2B SaaS collaborative product-planning workspace for product managers and product operations teams.

## Product Promise

AtlasBoard helps teams synthesize research, write evidence-linked PRDs, prioritize opportunities, turn approved PRDs into delivery plans, preview Linear and Notion syncs, prepare launches, and run post-launch learning.

## Primary Users

- Product managers who need to move from messy evidence to clear product decisions.
- Product operations leads who need consistent workflows and tool hygiene across PM teams.
- Product leaders who need confidence that roadmap bets are evidence-backed.

## Current Beta Scope

- Evidence import from markdown, CSV, and manual notes.
- Opportunity synthesis with source citations and missing-evidence labels.
- Prioritization workspace with configurable frameworks.
- PRD creation and review.
- Delivery breakdown into epics, stories, acceptance criteria, and dependencies.
- Linear and Notion dry-run preview payloads only.
- Launch readiness and post-launch learning artifacts.

## Out of Scope For This Synthetic Test

- Real customer data.
- Real API keys, secrets, or workspace IDs.
- Live writes to Notion, Linear, GitHub, npm, or any network system.
- Claims that AtlasBoard has completed security certifications.
