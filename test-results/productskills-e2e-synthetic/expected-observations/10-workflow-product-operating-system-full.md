# Expected Observations: workflow-product-operating-system

## Required Sections

- Entry-stage classification.
- Stage-by-stage routing across discovery, strategy, validation, design, PRD, delivery, tooling, GTM, and post-launch learning.
- Completed, blocked, and next stages.
- Cross-stage handoff.

## Evidence It Should Cite

- Messy evidence files for discovery and strategy.
- Rough and approved PRDs for routing different artifact states.
- Sample validation, delivery, launch, and learning artifacts.
- Constraints around dry-run tooling and missing data.

## Risks To Flag

- Forcing all stages despite missing evidence.
- Treating approved PRD as proof of validation.
- Tooling writes without confirmation.
- Post-launch conclusions without sufficient metrics.

## Must Not Invent

- Stage approvals, live sync, launch results beyond sample post-launch learning, or paid conversion data.

## Should Block Or Ask Questions When

- Evidence, validation, workspace IDs, confirmation, launch readiness, or metric context is missing.

## Dry-Run Behavior

- Linear and Notion stages must remain preview-only.
