# Sample Validation Decision

## Opportunity

Evidence-linked PRD and dry-run delivery preview for Notion plus Linear teams.

## Decision

Proceed to prototype and limited delivery planning, but block live external sync.

## Evidence Supporting Proceed

- INT-001, INT-002, INT-003, INT-005, and INT-006 show repeated pain around evidence traceability, prioritization confidence, and delivery handoff.
- SUP-002, SUP-005, and SALES-002 show urgent need for safer preview behavior.
- Usage analytics show Product Ops is the strongest activation and retention segment.

## Riskiest Assumptions

- Teams will trust generated artifacts if citations and missing-evidence labels are clear.
- Dry-run preview is valuable enough without live writes.
- Admin controls can satisfy enterprise evaluators.

## Validation Still Needed

- Usability test of dry-run preview comprehension.
- Security/admin-control review with enterprise evaluators.
- Pricing and willingness-to-pay research.
- Paid conversion instrumentation.

## Blocked Claims

- AtlasBoard has validated paid conversion.
- AtlasBoard can safely perform live external writes.
- AtlasBoard supports GitHub Issues preview.
