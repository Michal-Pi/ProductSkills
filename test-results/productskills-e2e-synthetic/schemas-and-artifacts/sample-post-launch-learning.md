# Sample Post-Launch Learning

## Launch

Linear/Notion Dry-Run Delivery Preview

## Synthetic Post-Launch Signals

- 18 Product Ops workspaces generated at least one preview.
- 12 of 18 preview users exported markdown artifacts.
- 5 users asked when live sync would be available.
- 4 users reported confusion between dry-run preview and completed sync.
- 3 enterprise evaluators asked for admin disable controls.

## Learnings

- Preview artifacts are valuable for review, but dry-run status needs stronger visual and textual treatment.
- Product Ops remains the strongest segment.
- Live sync demand exists but should remain blocked until workspace ID resolution, confirmation, admin controls, and idempotency maps are implemented.

## Next Decisions

- Improve dry-run comprehension before live sync development.
- Add admin-disabled state to launch criteria.
- Instrument preview-to-export and preview-to-return metrics.

## Missing Data

- No paid conversion results.
- No long-term retention beyond week 4.
- No direct support cost measurement.
