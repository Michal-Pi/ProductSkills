# Competitor Notes

All competitor analysis is synthetic and should not be treated as market fact.

## COMP-001 - Productboard-like feedback platform

- Strength: mature feedback capture, customer segmentation, roadmap portal.
- Weakness: users report heavy setup and limited delivery handoff flexibility.
- AtlasBoard implication: avoid competing as a generic feedback database; emphasize evidence-to-PRD-to-delivery continuity.

## COMP-002 - Notion AI templates

- Strength: cheap, flexible, already where PM docs live.
- Weakness: weak structured evidence traceability and no delivery-ready issue planning.
- AtlasBoard implication: must prove stronger decision quality, not just faster writing.

## COMP-003 - Jira Product Discovery-like planning tool

- Strength: prioritization connected to Jira delivery.
- Weakness: less compelling for Linear-first teams and teams using Notion for narrative PRDs.
- AtlasBoard implication: Notion plus Linear niche is plausible, but Jira-heavy enterprise will need a separate strategy.

## COMP-004 - Dovetail-like research repository

- Strength: deep research analysis and tagging.
- Weakness: handoff into PRDs and delivery plans remains manual.
- AtlasBoard implication: integrate or import research summaries rather than replace research repositories.
