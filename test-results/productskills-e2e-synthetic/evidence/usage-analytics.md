## Funnel Definitions

- Activation: workspace imports at least 10 evidence items, creates at least 3 opportunities, and creates or reviews at least 1 PRD within 7 days.
- Retention: workspace has at least 2 active users and one meaningful artifact action in week 4.
- External writes: disabled during beta, so all confirmed external write counts are intentionally zero.

| Funnel Step | Overall Conversion From Previous Step |
| --- | ---: |

## Cohort Notes

- Product Ops teams have the highest activation and retention.
- SMB teams import less evidence and rarely use previews.
- Mid-market teams use evidence-linked PRDs more than enterprise teams by volume.
- No data exists yet for paid conversion, expansion, or long-term retention beyond week 4.
