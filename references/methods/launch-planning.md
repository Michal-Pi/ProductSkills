# Launch Planning

Use launch planning when a product change needs customer, GTM, support, or internal coordination.

## Launch Readiness Areas

- Audience and segmentation
- Positioning and messaging
- Customer impact
- Channel plan
- Internal enablement
- Support readiness
- Legal, privacy, or policy review
- Rollout plan
- Success metrics
- Post-launch learning loop

## Launch Types

- Silent release: low-risk change with internal notes only.
- Beta: controlled learning with known participants.
- Limited release: staged rollout to a segment.
- General availability: broad launch with support, sales, and customer communication.
- Deprecation: customer migration, support plan, and timeline.

## Guardrails

- Do not over-launch small changes.
- Do not launch externally without support and customer impact review.
- Do not omit rollback or mitigation plans for risky releases.
