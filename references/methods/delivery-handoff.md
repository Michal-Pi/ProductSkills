# Delivery Handoff

Use delivery handoffs to preserve product intent while creating buildable work.

## Good Handoffs Include

- Outcome and target user
- Evidence and assumptions
- Scope and non-goals
- Epics or story map
- Acceptance criteria
- Edge cases and failure states
- Dependencies
- Release or rollout constraints
- Open product decisions

## Splitting Guidance

- Split by user outcome before splitting by technical layer.
- Keep stories independently valuable when possible.
- Put unknowns into spikes only when they answer a named decision.
- Keep acceptance criteria observable and testable.

## Guardrails

- Do not create implementation tasks from vague product intent.
- Do not drop non-goals, risks, or open questions during handoff.
- Do not prepare external tool writes without a preview.
