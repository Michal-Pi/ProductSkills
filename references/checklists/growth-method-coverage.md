# Growth Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing growth outputs.

- Growth model, funnel, or loop is named.
- Target segment and metric are explicit.
- Activation, retention, monetization, lifecycle, PLG, and distribution loops are considered where relevant.
- Bottleneck is separated from symptoms.
- Hypotheses are tied to evidence or assumptions.
- Experiments include success metric, guardrail, audience, duration, and decision threshold.
- Output avoids optimizing acquisition when activation or retention is the binding constraint.
