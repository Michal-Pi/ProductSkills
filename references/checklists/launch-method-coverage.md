# Launch Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing launch or GTM artifacts.

- Launch type is explicit: silent release, beta, limited release, general availability, or deprecation.
- Audience, customer impact, positioning, and proof are clear.
- Channels, timing, owners, and enablement needs are named.
- Support, legal, privacy, policy, or migration needs are checked where relevant.
- Rollout, mitigation, and manual fallback are included for risky launches.
- Success metrics and post-launch review date are defined.
- Release notes avoid unsupported claims or roadmap promises.
