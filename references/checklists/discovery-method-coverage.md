# Discovery Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist before considering discovery work complete.

- Customer evidence is separated from internal opinion and inference.
- Source type and confidence are labeled.
- Customer job, pain, trigger, workaround, and desired outcome are considered.
- Opportunity framing starts from outcome, not solution.
- Assumptions are named and routed to validation when material.
- Thin evidence results in research questions or a research plan.
- Continuous discovery and opportunity solution tree concepts are used when decisions are ongoing.
