# Docs Quality Checklist

Use this checklist when drafting or reviewing PRDs, specs, and product decisions.

- Objective, customer, problem, evidence, assumptions, scope, and non-goals are present.
- Evidence is separated from inference and stakeholder opinion.
- Success metrics and guardrails are measurable.
- Requirements are testable without over-specifying implementation.
- Risks, open questions, and decision owners are explicit.
- Next actions are concrete.
- Tool payloads are not implied unless routed through `pm-tooling`.
