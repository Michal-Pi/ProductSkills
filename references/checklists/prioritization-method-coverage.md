# Prioritization Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing prioritization outputs.

- The decision type is explicit: ranking, sequencing, release scope, experiment backlog, or strategic tradeoff.
- RICE, WSJF, cost of delay, CD3, ICE, opportunity scoring, weighted scorecards, Kano, and MoSCoW were considered where relevant.
- Criteria and weights are visible before scores are interpreted.
- Confidence is attached to inputs, not just final score.
- Close calls include sensitivity or tie-break notes.
- Recommendation includes risks and next action.
- The output avoids pretending the scoring model is objective truth.
