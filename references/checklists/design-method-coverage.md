# Design Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing design briefs, prototype specs, and usability plans.

- Target user, scenario, and user goal are explicit.
- Scope and non-goals are named.
- Primary flow, secondary states, empty states, errors, permissions, and edge cases are covered.
- Content, data, and instrumentation needs are visible.
- Prototype fidelity matches the decision being tested.
- Usability tasks avoid leading the participant.
- Output routes unresolved product assumptions to validation.
