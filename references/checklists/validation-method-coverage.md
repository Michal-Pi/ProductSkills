# Validation Method Coverage Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing validation plans.

- Decision and riskiest assumption are explicit.
- Assumptions cover desirability, viability, feasibility, usability, GTM, and compliance where relevant.
- Test method is the smallest credible way to answer the decision.
- Audience, success metric, guardrail metric, and decision rule are defined.
- Evidence confidence is named.
- False-positive risks and invalidation signals are included.
- Result can lead to ship, iterate, research, narrow, or stop.
