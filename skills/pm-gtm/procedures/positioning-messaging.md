# Positioning and Messaging

Use when defining how a product change should be explained to the market or internal teams.

## Steps

1. Identify audience, problem, alternative, and value.
2. State positioning: for whom, who need, this is, that provides, unlike.
3. Draft primary message and proof points.
4. Tailor by channel or stakeholder group.
5. Identify claims that need evidence or legal review.
6. Define next review owner.

## Output

Return positioning, message, proof, channel variants, risks, and open questions.
