# Opportunity Framing

Use when turning discovery evidence into product opportunities.

## Steps

1. Start with a measurable product or customer outcome.
2. List customer opportunities supported by evidence.
3. Map each opportunity to user segment, job, pain, current workaround, and confidence.
4. Identify solution ideas separately from opportunities.
5. Name assumptions for the most promising opportunities.
6. Route to `pm-validation` for riskiest assumption testing or `pm-docs` for PRD drafting when evidence is strong.

## Output

Return outcome, opportunity list, evidence, assumptions, and recommended route.
