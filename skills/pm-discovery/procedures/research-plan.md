# Research Plan

Use when evidence is thin or a product decision needs customer learning.

## Steps

1. State the product decision the research should inform.
2. Name what must be learned and what is already known.
3. Choose method using `../../../references/methods/research-methods.md`.
4. Define target participants and recruiting criteria.
5. Draft questions or tasks that avoid leading the participant.
6. Define synthesis and decision criteria.

## Output

Use `../../../templates/research-plan.md`. Return research objective, method, audience, questions, timeline, risks, and decision use.
