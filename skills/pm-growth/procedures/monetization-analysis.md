# Monetization Analysis

Use when improving conversion, expansion, pricing, packaging, or revenue quality.

## Steps

1. Define monetization objective and segment.
2. Map value received, willingness to pay, buyer role, and pricing metric.
3. Identify conversion or expansion bottleneck.
4. Check whether the issue is value, packaging, timing, trust, procurement, or price.
5. Propose monetization tests with guardrails.
6. Define revenue metric, customer-impact guardrail, and decision rule.

## Output

Return monetization diagnosis, evidence, hypotheses, experiments, and risks.
