# Usability Test Plan

Use when testing whether users can understand and complete a product flow.

## Steps

1. State test objective and target user.
2. Define scenario and tasks.
3. Identify success criteria, friction signals, and observation notes.
4. Include required prototype state and test data.
5. Avoid leading instructions.
6. Define synthesis and decision rule.
