# OKR Setting

Use when translating product strategy into objectives and measurable key results.

## Steps

1. Clarify company or product outcome.
2. Identify customer and business constraints.
3. Draft one focused objective.
4. Create measurable key results with baseline, target, owner, and time horizon.
5. Identify initiatives separately from key results.
6. Check for metric gaming and guardrails.

## Output

Return objective, key results, rationale, guardrails, initiatives, and review cadence.
