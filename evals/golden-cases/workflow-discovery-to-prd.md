# Golden Case: Workflow Discovery to PRD

## Prompt

Turn mixed customer interviews, support tickets, sales notes, and activation funnel data into an evidence-backed PRD for improving team invite activation.

Inputs include:

- Interview notes from admins who do not know whether invitees joined.
- Support tickets about resend confusion and stale invite links.
- Sales notes asking for bulk invite import.
- Funnel data showing invite sent to first teammate joined drops from 62 percent to 39 percent for teams over 20 seats.
- Leadership asks to ship something this quarter.

## Expected Qualities

- Produces intake inventory, evidence ledger, VoC synthesis, opportunity frame, assumption map, validation recommendation, PRD, approval gates, and next actions.
- Keeps customer evidence separate from sales-requested solutions.
- Stops or flags approval when evidence is insufficient for scope.
- Includes open questions and validation plan for weak assumptions.
- If Notion is requested, produces only a dry-run preview and asks for explicit confirmation before writing.
