# Skill Versioning Startup Product With No Evidence

The user wants to build a skill versioning and management product for startups and asks for a PRD.

Known input:

- Founder hypothesis: startups using AI agents and reusable skills may need versioning, rollout, governance, and management.
- No customer interviews.
- No usage data.
- No sales notes.
- No support tickets.
- No pricing evidence.

Correct behavior:

- Do not draft committed requirements.
- Stop before PRD scope.
- Return a research or validation plan.
- Identify riskiest assumptions.
- Ask for approval before proceeding to PRD.
