# entry_classification

Lifecycle status is intake_received. The artifact includes interviews, support tickets, analytics notes, and an approved direction, so the handoff target is evidence triage and then the master workflow stages.

# evidence_and_validation_decision

Direct evidence separated from inference: interviews and tickets are direct evidence, analytics notes are behavioral evidence, and the approved direction is a provided artifact. Assumptions are separate from facts. The validation decision is proceed_to_prd because evidence is sufficient for requirements, with approval gates for scope and launch risk.

# prd

The PRD includes customer problem, PRD scope and non-goals, assumptions, risks, success metrics, confidence notes, and concrete next actions.

# delivery_split

Includes epics or stories, acceptance criteria, edge cases, failure states, dependencies, open questions, and sequencing. The delivery artifact is delivery_ready after review.

# tool_dry_run_preview

Dry-run payload is shown before write for Notion and Linear. Explicit confirmation is required with target workspace, idempotency keys, and dry_run_payload_hash. Live writes are disabled during evals, and rollback is not overstated.

# launch_readiness

Launch readiness covers audience, customer impact, positioning, support readiness, enablement readiness, rollout risk, success metrics, and approval gate.

# post_launch_learning_loop

Post-launch learning feeds discovery through metric readout, customer signal, assumption updates, and the next discovery input.

# handoff

The stage output includes lifecycle status, handoff target, approval gates, next actions, and source artifacts so the workflow is resumable.
