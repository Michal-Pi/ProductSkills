# entry_classification

Lifecycle status is ready_for_launch_review because delivery artifacts already exist and the user asks for launch readiness.

# validation_not_required

Validation not required for this re-entry. The readiness review records evidence and product risks without forcing discovery replay.

# launch_readiness_gate

Launch readiness reviews customer impact, rollout risk, approval gates, and handoff target. Direct evidence is the provided delivery artifact and launch request; missing evidence is any absent support baseline. Assumptions are separate from facts.

# audience

Audience includes self-serve admins, internal support, sales, and customer success.

# support_readiness

Support readiness includes known issue handling, escalation owner, help content, and support response plan.

# enablement_readiness

Enablement readiness includes release notes, positioning, customer-facing messaging, internal FAQ, and owner training.

# success_metrics

Success metrics include adoption, activation, support ticket volume, and guardrail metric readout.

# post_launch_learning_plan

Post-launch learning includes metric review window, customer signal collection, assumption updates, next discovery input, and concrete next actions.
