# Research Plan

## Decision

What product decision this research should inform.

## Current Evidence

Known evidence, source quality, and missing evidence.

## Assumptions

Untested desirability, viability, feasibility, usability, GTM, and compliance assumptions.

## Riskiest Assumption

The assumption most likely to invalidate the next product commitment.

## Research Method

Interview, survey, prototype test, concierge test, landing page test, analytics review, or other method, with rationale.

## Audience

Target segment, recruiting criteria, exclusions, and sample constraints.

## Questions Or Tasks

Non-leading questions or tasks.

## Success Metric

Signal that supports moving forward.

## Guardrail Metric

Signal that prevents over-optimizing or causing harm.

## Decision Rule

What result means proceed, revise, research more, or stop.

## Timeline

Timebox and sequencing.

## Next Action

The immediate next step and owner when known.
